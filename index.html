04

How I work

The soft skills that decide whether the hard ones matter.

USERS_FIRSTResponsibility scales with reach

I think critically about what I'm building and how it shapes real lives. Reach comes with responsibility for the impact — the good and the bad — and I'd rather get that right than ship faster.

PRAGMATICOpinionated, not ideological

Languages and frameworks are tradeoffs, not religions. I have strong views, hold them loosely, and pick up whatever the problem actually needs — then learn it well.

COMMSClear about the hard parts

I explain technical decisions in plain language, in writing or live. In disagreement I engage with the other view honestly and compromise when it serves the work.

TEAMBuilding is not a solo act

I like working across design, product, and engineering toward a shared goal. I care about learning and growing — and about helping the people around me do the same.

Automating the busy work so people get more time for their life's work — from a business's system of record to the AI agents that run alongside it.
